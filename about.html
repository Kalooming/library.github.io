About

Hello — I'm Philoskus. This site collects short essays, notes, and reading commentary on topics in philosophy: metaphysics, ethics, language, and the craft of conceptual clarity. The format is intentionally simple so ideas come first.

How I write

I try to keep pieces short and focused. Each essay contains a few numbered sections, a brief bibliography if needed, and links to further reading. If you'd like me to add an email/contact link or a newsletter signup, I can add that.
