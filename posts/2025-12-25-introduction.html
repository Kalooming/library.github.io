A Short Introduction to the Project

Dec 25, 2025

1. Why write

I write to clarify ideas for myself and for readers who enjoy concise, concept-driven essays. The aim is clarity rather than novelty for novelty's sake.

2. What you will find

Short essays, reading notes, and occasional longer treatments. Topics will frequently touch on metaphysics, epistemology, ethics, and philosophy of language.

3. How to cite

Prefer the page URL and date in citations. If you reuse material, please attribute the author (Philoskus).
